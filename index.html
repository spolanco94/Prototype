General Information for Visitors

Power & Electricity

Taniti uses 120-volt outlets (same as the U.S.). No adapter is needed for American visitors.

Alcohol & Drinking Laws

Alcohol sales are prohibited from midnight to 9:00 a.m. The drinking age is 18 but not strictly enforced.

Language

English is widely spoken, but older residents in rural areas may not be fluent.

Medical Facilities

There is one hospital and several clinics with multilingual staff.

Safety

Violent crime is rare, but petty theft (pickpocketing) has increased due to tourism.

Currency

Taniti uses U.S. dollars, but many businesses accept euros & yen. Credit cards are widely accepted.
